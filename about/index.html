About the homepage

My homepage shows various examples of what are known as 2D cellular automata. You can interact with the simulations with your keyboard. Press 'p' to pause, and 'r' to reset. Use your left and right arrows to move between rules, and 's' to stay on the current rule. If you would like to learn more about cellular automata, here are some online resources.

  • The Wikipedia entry for cellular automata
  • This Mathworld article gives a brief technical overview
  • This entry in the Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy gives a much longer overview from a philosophy of science point of view
  • This page explains the rule encoding displayed on the home page
  • This pdf is a technical paper by Stephen Wolfram who has investigated cellular automata for many years
  • This enormous and free online book, also by Stephen Wolfram
