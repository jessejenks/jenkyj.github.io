About the homepage

My homepage shows various examples of what are known as 2D cellular automata. You can interact with the simulations with your keyboard. Press 'p' to pause, and 'r' to reset. Use your left and right arrows to move between rules, and 's' to stay on the current rule. If you would like to learn more about cellular automata, here are some online resources.

  • {% include link href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cellular_automata" content='The Wikipedia entry for cellular automata' %}
  • {% include link href="http://mathworld.wolfram.com/CellularAutomaton.html" content="This Mathworld article gives a brief technical overview" %}
  • {% include link href="https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/cellular-automata/" content="This entry in the Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy" %} gives a much longer overview from a philosophy of science point of view
  • {% include link href="http://www.mirekw.com/ca/rullex_life.html" content="This page explains the rule encoding displayed on the home page." %}
  • {% include link href="https://www.wolframscience.com/nks/" content="This enormous and free online book" %}
